Enhance the Django tutor management system by adding FULL multi-timezone support for international students.

---

## MULTI-TIMEZONE SUPPORT (IMPORTANT)

The system must support students from different countries (Europe, USA, etc.) and provide a clear, user-friendly way to handle time differences during scheduling.

---

1. Student Timezone & Country

---

Extend the Student model:

* Add fields:

  * country (string or selectable list)
  * timezone (IANA timezone, e.g. "Europe/Berlin", "America/New_York")

* The tutor is always in:

  * timezone = Africa/Cairo

---

2. Time Storage Strategy (CRITICAL)

---

* ALL datetime values must be stored in UTC in the database

* Convert:

  * from tutor timezone (Africa/Cairo) → UTC when saving
  * from UTC → user timezone when displaying

* Use Django timezone utilities properly

* DO NOT use naive datetime

---

3. Scheduling Logic (Timezone-Aware)

---

When scheduling a session:

* Tutor selects time in Cairo timezone
* System automatically:

  * converts and displays equivalent time in student's timezone

Example:

* Tutor selects: 6:00 PM Cairo
* Student sees: 11:00 AM New York

---

4. UI/UX – Time Difference Visualization (CREATIVE & IMPORTANT)

---

When creating or viewing a session:

* Show BOTH times clearly:

  ## Example UI:

  Tutor Time (Cairo):
  🕕 18:00

  Student Time (New York):
  🕚 11:00

  Time Difference:
  -7 hours
  --------

* Display:

  * student country (flag or name)
  * timezone label

* Make it visually clear and modern:

  * badges / icons / color distinction

---

5. Smart Scheduling with Timezones

---

* Ensure:

  * no overlap is checked in UTC (global consistency)

* While suggesting available slots:

  * consider:

    * tutor working hours (Cairo time)
    * student timezone for display ONLY

---

6. Prayer Time Integration (CRITICAL)

---

* Prayer times apply ONLY to tutor timezone (Cairo)

* Blocking logic:

  * computed in Cairo time
  * then converted to UTC for validation

* Ensure:

  * timezone differences DO NOT break prayer blocking

---

7. Validation Rules

---

* Prevent:

  * overlapping sessions (UTC-based)
  * booking during blocked prayer intervals
  * booking outside working hours

* Ensure:

  * timezone conversion is consistent everywhere

---

8. Calendar View (ENHANCED)

---

* Main calendar is in tutor timezone (Cairo)

* OPTIONAL (nice UX):

  * show small label inside each event:
    → student local time

Example:
"Ali – 6:00 PM (Cairo) / 11:00 AM (NY)"

---

9. Analytics Impact

---

* Add optional analytics:

  * sessions by country
  * busiest regions

---

10. Edge Cases (IMPORTANT)

---

Handle correctly:

* Daylight Saving Time (DST)
* Students changing timezone
* Sessions crossing midnight in different zones

---

* Use Django timezone utilities:

  * django.utils.timezone

* Ensure:

  * all conversions are centralized (helper/service)
